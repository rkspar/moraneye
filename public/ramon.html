Dr. Ramon Moran Jr.

Dr. Ramon Moran Jr. is the founder and senior partner of the Eye Care Center. A native of Miami, Dr. Moran studied his undergraduate at the University of Florida and received his Doctor of Optometry degree from the Illinois College of Optometry in Chicago. Clinically, he has special interest in contact lenses and refractive surgery.

An active member of the Dade County, Florida, and American Optometric Association, Dr. Moran resides in Brickell with his wife, Miki, and enjoys traveling and spending time with his grandchildren.
